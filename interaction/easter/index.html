EASTER EGG COLLECTION

What are easter eggs?

Easter eggs are forms of messages, images, or features that are typically hidden in software, video games, film, or other electronic mediums. This term was first coined by Steve Wright in 1979 after the most prominent easter egg in histroy which was held in Atari.

Importance

The importance of easter eggs comes from its personal value, as many of these hidden features contain messages that are filled with fun gimmicks and jokes that records the creator's passion. Many of these records are still hidden from the world and it is up to the audience to discover them and piece them together. This website is a way to contain the history of previously hidden eggs that had made an impact to the entertainment industry.
